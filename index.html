The GeoComputation community has centred itself around a series of international research conferences initiated in 1996. From 2002 GeoComp has alternated every alternate year with GIScience. The conference moves to a different location each time. This site maintains papers and other information from previous conferences and links to upcoming conferences.

Upcoming

We're delighted to announce that GeoComputation 2019 will be in Queenstown, New Zealand. The conference will be on the 18th to the 21st Sept. 2019. For more details of this and associated events, see the Conference Website (note change in address).
